Hero Fighting Game

How to play:

1. Both players choose a hero by searching like "batman" or "spider-man" or they can generate a random hero

2. Players can see Heros statistics: intelligence for heal, strength for attack power and durability for health points value

3. Player 1 attack with "Q" and heal with "A" keys

4. Player 2 attack with "P" and heal with "L" keys

5. Direct fight only on desktop version. Players can simulate the combat on mobile!
